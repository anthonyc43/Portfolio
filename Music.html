Music

I began my music production journey during the 2024–2025 academic year, serving as music producer for three video game projects: "Fightatron", "The Secret Of Sweetshade Industry" and "Echo Chambers." Music production has become a central component of my creative portfolio, enabling me to integrate my passion for gaming with artistic expression and technical skill. I utilize audio production software such as Soundtrap to compose original tracks, create atmospheric sound effects, and edit audio assets. The experience has refined my understanding of audio design principles and their application to interactive media. I am committed to further developing my expertise in music composition and sound design.
